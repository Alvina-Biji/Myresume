Projects

1. Effect of electronic gadgets on a student’s academic performance

Examined the impact of electronic gadgets on students’ academic performance. Through data collection and statistical analysis, this exploratory data analysis project aimed to uncover any potential relationships between gadget usage patterns and academic achievements.

2. Airline Reviews:Sentiment Analysis

Conducted a sentiment analysis of British airline quality reviews by web scrapping using natural language processing.

3. Application of bio-medical statistics on epidemiological progression on cholera:a data science perspective

Summarized India’s need for strengthened disease surveillance, particularly for cholera.Visualized the patterns and trends associated with cholera in India.Also gathered data on the duration of cholera outbreaks, examined their correlation with water bodies, and delved into the analysis of the disease.

4. Indian forestry products: An exploratory data analysis approach

Investigated the trends and patterns in the production and consumption of various forestry products in India using FAOSTAT data utilizing Python.

5. Tracking: A spatial-temporal approach

Reviewed visual tracking method called the spatial-temporal and regular correlation filter (STRCF) and studied the results of spatial and temporal regularizations. STRCF was evaluated against the standard OTB-2015 benchmarks dataset.
